Pupusa

Description: This dish originates in the region of el salvador. Traditionaly served with meat and lettuce, today we will give it an exciting twist. Instead we will be using bean and cheese for the filling and smothereing each pupusa in zesty salsa verde.

Ingredients

  • Spelt flour
  • Garbanzo beans
  • Buffalo mozzarela
  • Tomatillos
  • Cilantro
  • Red Onions
  • Water
  • Seasoning and Salt

Steps

  1. "Turn the four into dough by mixing half the amount of flour in water and a pinch of salt. Kneed until dough is supple."
  2. "In a sauce pan heat up garbanzo beans and onions for a couple minutes, once soft add water and mash until refried beans consistency."
  3. "Blend tomatillos, onion, cilantro and salt till desired consistency for salsa verde."
  4. "Lastly cut dough into balls and fill with buffalo mozzarela and refried beans. Once filled close the dough, flaten it out as flat as possible and soft fry in a pan with some oil."
  5. " In a bowl add pupusa and cover with salsa verde, Enjoy!
